Quick Find

Designed to facilitate the navigation of your subscription by enabling you to easily find where the currently effective pronouncements or practice management exhibits are located.

Navigating By Document Type

  • You can quickly navigate to a document by clicking on the applicable type of pronouncement (or other document) from the document type list on the left side of the screen
  • A second list will be returned of all currently effective documents in a given category
  • Navigate to the original pronouncement you are looking to find, and click on the link
  • If you currently subscribe to the title where that document is located, you will be taken to the appropriate section of the reSOURCE Library (if you do not subscribe to the title where the document is located, you will receive an error message)

Navigating By Subject Matter

  • Simply select the applicable category from the subject matter list on the left side of the screen and follow the links.

Additional Tips

Documents that are not included in your subscription will have a    displayed to the left of the document title. By clicking on the link, you will have the opportunity to subscribe to the referenced title.

You can open and close the Document Type or Subject window by clicking on the arrow  at top right of the window.
