Navigating the Table of Contents

When you first open the Table of Contents, it shows the first level of documents or folders in the site. Click the + next to the headings in the Table of Contents to expand the headings and the – next to the headings in the Table of Contents to contract the headings. You may view a document by clicking the document name in the Table of Contents. The document appears in the document frame next to the Table of Contents.

Expanding the Table of Contents View

To see a wider view of the Table of Contents, click on the   button at the top of the Table of Contents. To narrow the view, click on the   button again.

Copy/Paste

As you view pages on the site, you will find information that you might want to save for future reference or use in a report or documentation. To save a selection of text:

  • Select the information you want to copy by holding down your left mouse button and dragging over the material you want to save.
  • From your browser's toolbar, click the Edit menu, and then click Copy.
  • With the appropriate document open in your word processing program, click the Edit menu from your browser's toolbar, and then click Paste.





Common Error Messages

Access Denied

There are a number of circumstances that will cause this message to display. The basic reason surrounding each of the instances is that you do not subscribe to the title that you are attempting to link to. In most circumstances, you will be given an option to return to where you were before you clicked on the link or follow a link to purchase a subscription to the title that you were trying to access.


Duplicate User

This message will come up when 2 people attempt to log into the site with the same username and password at the same time.


Firm Limit Exceeded

This message will come up when you have a firm subscription, and all of the "seats" subscribed to are being simultaneously used by other members of your firm. If you get this message, try logging in again later.


Session Time-Out

You may encounter circumstances when you receive a session time-out message. Sometimes refreshing the screen from your brower’s Refresh button will return you to the document you were in. In other cases, it will be necessary to logout of the product and the CPA2Biz site, wait a little while, and log back in.
